BRANDING, WIREFRAMES, MOCKUPS, FRONT-END

Project

A showcase of any product of my wish. I chose to do a concept car by Bentley. Showcasing the aspects of the car and to entice users to possibly purchase the car.

Target

After some web searches, I came across the EXP 10 Speed 6 a car that many people have been petitioning to make it's conception become an actual model for Bentley. Thus, I took the conceptual car and created a way for possible purchasers to gain further information on the car.

Solution

Researched the history of Bentley and the idea of their concept car. Eventually generating an identity for the car by creating it's own dedicated logo, wireframes, high fidelty mock-ups of the landing page, and developed a responsive site for users to become engrossed with the car.
