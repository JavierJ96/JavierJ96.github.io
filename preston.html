ALLAN

BRANDING, WEB DESIGN, PHOTOGRAPHY

Project

With another student, create their own musical alter ego. ALLAN is an R&B artist from Brooklyn, NY. ALLAN'S original name is Preston.

Target

Fans of ALLAN are able to get a good overview of him, get updates on about his current life, and find out about upcoming tour dates.

Solution

By working one on one, create an entire persona for ALLAN. From that persona, showcase ALLAN as musical artist and correctly represent him as his own brand. Portrait photography taken with studio equipment, then completing logo creation, a color palette and original album cover creation. Eventually creating a mock-up desktop site for him as well.
