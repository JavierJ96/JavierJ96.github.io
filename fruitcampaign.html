CRUNCH TIME

PRINT ADVERTISEMENT, PHOTOGRAPHY

Project

A group advertisement campaign to increase awareness about eating healthy via fruits.

Target

After much debate, kids in grade school to college students were the main age group to present that eating fruits will always be great.

Solution

In my team of 3, I was tasked with making a print deliverable for our client. As a team, we brainstormed and concluded with a humourous idea by physically biting into the fruits with the skin on it. As my task, I took the photography using studio equipment, edited the images and accented the fruits by using complimentary colors to create appealling posters.
