REDOLENCE

Shopping at it's finest.

Project

Created a beautiful, yet simple online shopping experience for all shapes and sizes. A total package with a creative homepage, browsing products, viewing products, adding a review and eventually purchasing.

Challenge

Organize and prioritize information, hence users couldn't become overwhelmed. Also, needed to create an atmosphere so both men and women can feel comfortable shopping on.

Designing mobile-first is a priority.

Approach

The main goal was to ease a shoppers a experience by making content easily accessible and visually calming, without losing functionality. Beginning with a competitive analysis and wireframes for layout. Eventually, by creating an aura of the brand through logo creation and a dedicated color palette.

Sizes, shapes, and more sizes.
