RENOTE

BRANDING, SURVEYS, PERSONAS, FLOWS, WIREFRAMES, MOCK-UPS, FRONT-END

Project

Renote is a SaaS (Software as a Service) web application which allows people to collect information and store it in a single place. A person can create simple documents, store images, links to websites, and collaborate with others.

Target

After completing user surveys, many are high school students to college level reaching the age of 30. Job occupations include creatives who love to collaborate and share ideas. Also, for many business people that need organization in there life.

Solution

Beginning with a concept, it started with user surveys, creating user personas, user flows and a competitve analysis. Eventually wireframing the homepage and dashboard and completing user testing. Then, with logo creation and a color palette. Then ultimately, coding a responsive homepage and dashboard.
